Accessibility Theme

A theme with black, white and greyscale icons with high contrast and small hints of colour. These are pure SVG icons, therefore don't come in different size variants, like 64 pixels to 256 pixels square.

The program icons are the same, and the jQueryUI CSS/JavaScript has modifications such as larger amounts for padding and text, and the possibility of slower animations. I'll use no green or red in this theme, because some people are colour blind. Keyboard shortcuts using alt and otherwise will be included anyway.
